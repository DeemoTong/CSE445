This is a Windows Forms  Application for CDYNE Weather service that allows you to get the seven day forecast of by zipcode of United States.

How to use: input a five-digit ZIP code, then click the Get Forecast! button or press Enter, you will get the weather information of the corresponding region, including  a general description, probability of precipiation, and lowest and highest temperature. The location name represented by the ZIP code will also be displayed.

WSDL Address:	http://ws.cdyne.com/WeatherWS/Weather.asmx?wsdl
